[Organisation name] confirms its support for the above application and its willingness, subject to the usual local approvals, to act as a delivery and recruitment site for the proposed feasibility study.

We understand and accept that it does not diagnose, prescribe, treat, provide therapy, provide crisis care or make safeguarding decisions, and that our clinical and service teams retain full responsibility for those functions throughout the study period.

[Organisation name] is willing, in principle, to:

  • provide reasonable staff time for briefing and study support, as scoped in the study protocol;
  • allow the study team to pressure-test the OTOS Continuity™ model against our real pathway, within the agreed governance boundary;
  • participate in study governance discussions relevant to our site.

This letter confirms support in principle only. A full site agreement, information governance review and local approvals will follow, ahead of any study activity beginning at our site.
