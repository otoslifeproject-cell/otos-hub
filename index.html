About the Hub

What the OTOS Hub is, what lives where, and how to use it day to day.
What this is

The OTOS Hub is the shared internal workspace for Dean and Gareth — the single place all OTOS documents, contacts and outreach are tracked. It lives behind the Cloudflare gate at hub.otos.network; nothing here is public.

Everything you change saves automatically for both of you. Add a contact, tick a document, complete a task, leave a note — it writes live to the shared database and the other person sees it on their next refresh. There is nothing to "push" or "save to GitHub" for any of it.

TodayYour landing screen. The next actions that matter, overdue and stalled items, and a one-click daily digest. Start here each morning.
TasksThe shared job list. Add anything, assign an owner, set a due date & time — you'll get a reminder toast when it's due. Board and list views.
ContactsEveryone in the plan — academic, local NHS/partner, national. Open a record for its history, tasks, notes and next action. Search, tag and filter.

Getting things done — the quick way

Press ⌘K (or Ctrl-K) anywhere to jump to any screen or contact. The ＋ button (bottom-right) quick-adds a task or contact from anywhere. On the phone, swipe a task right to mark it done, and tap any phone number to call.

Read this first — why OTOS is not the applicant

On every route in this pack, OTOS Digital Ltd is not the applicant. The MRC and Wellcome both fund eligible academic or NHS research organisations — so an academic or clinical academic leads each application, holds the grant and sponsors the ethics approval.

OTOS Digital Ltd sits as project partner, intervention owner and technology partner: we provide the model and the technology being studied, not the research governance. Service partners (e.g. NHS trusts, addiction or wellbeing services) support recruitment and delivery where agreed. Full detail in 03 — OTOS Role as Project Partner.

This holds on the fourth, earlier route too: on any Cambridge internal translational / Impact Acceleration-style funding, a Cambridge academic or department would lead, not OTOS — see 01 — Funding Route Summary.

One thing to know about live sync: all the working data — contacts, tasks, G/D/Ready ticks, owners, notes, statuses, dates and the activity feed — syncs live between Dean and Gareth automatically. The only thing that isn't live is the content of the document files themselves (their actual copy); editing those still happens outside the Hub and is published when a version is signed off.

Daily & weekly email digest

The Hub emails a short digest of what's due — a daily one each morning and a weekly summary on Mondays — automatically, to the people below. It's the only email the Hub sends, and it only ever goes to you two; the Hub never emails contacts. Change who receives it here anytime — it saves live, no redeploy needed.
