OTOS Role as Project Partner

OTOS Digital Ltd is not, and should not be presented as, the MRC lead applicant on any of these routes.

Governing principleAn eligible academic or NHS research organisation leads. OTOS is project partner. Service partners support delivery.
01

The three-tier structure

TierWhoResponsible for
Lead applicantEligible academic / NHS research organisationGrant holding, ethics sponsorship, research governance, academic direction, reporting to funder
Project partnerOTOS Digital LtdThe intervention itself — technology, non-clinical model, lived-experience origin, implementation support
Service partnersNHS / third-sector delivery sites (e.g. CPFT, RCE, CGL, Public Health teams)Recruitment, pressure-testing, delivery access, where agreed per site
02

Why OTOS does not lead

MRC and Wellcome routes are built for eligible research organisations, not for a small technology company acting alone. Presenting OTOS as the lead would misstate the routes' own eligibility logic and would weaken, not strengthen, an application. OTOS's contribution is the intervention and the lived-experience origin of the model — not the research infrastructure, ethics sponsorship or academic governance a funder is checking for.

03

What "project partner" means in practice

  • OTOS Digital Ltd owns and maintains the intervention (the Continuity Management Layer) throughout the study.
  • OTOS contributes named technical and product input to study design, without directing the research question.
  • OTOS does not hold the grant, does not sponsor ethics, and is not the accountable body to the funder.
  • OTOS's commercial interest is disclosed as a matter of course, consistent with standard funder conflict-of-interest requirements.
04

Data and IP, at a glance

Research data generated in the study (attendance, engagement and drift signals collected via OTOS) is governed by the lead institution's data governance and ethics approval. The underlying OTOS technology and non-clinical model remain OTOS Digital Ltd's intellectual property; research findings about its use are not. Full data-flow and governance detail belongs in the study protocol and data management plan, not this note — see 05.

Boundary

OTOS Continuity™ is non-clinical throughout. It surfaces signals; the clinical and service teams make the decisions.
