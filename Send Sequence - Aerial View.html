The drip feed — what "opening up bit by bit" means

The same person can move rightward through these rungs as trust builds. The Cloudflare gate is the hinge between rung 2 and rung 3: it lets you grant a warmed academic a real look at the working site without putting anything in front of the world — you open the gate one visitor at a time.
