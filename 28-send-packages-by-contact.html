Send packages by contact

Exactly what goes in the envelope for every audience across the whole OTOS family — not just which doc, but the companions sent alongside it, and what's held back for a follow-up once there's a real conversation. See 27 for when each phase opens.

The rule this applies

First contact = the partner's own brief, plus The Science of Sticking where the science reinforces the ask (it's science-led, not operating detail — safe pre-NDA). The Evidence Dossier is held back from a true first email — it's positioned as the evidence/technical appendix, sent once a meeting is agreed or interest is shown, exactly as already done for Public Health (sent because a first meeting had already happened). Sending it cold with a first-contact email jumps that ladder.
