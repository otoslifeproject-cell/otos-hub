Read this first — why OTOS is not the applicant

On every route in this pack, OTOS Digital Ltd is not the applicant. The MRC and Wellcome both fund eligible academic or NHS research organisations — so an academic or clinical academic leads each application, holds the grant and sponsors the ethics approval.

OTOS Digital Ltd sits as project partner, intervention owner and technology partner: we provide the model and the technology being studied, not the research governance. Service partners (e.g. NHS trusts, addiction or wellbeing services) support recruitment and delivery where agreed. Full detail in 03 — OTOS Role as Project Partner.

This holds on the fourth, earlier route too: on any Cambridge internal translational / Impact Acceleration-style funding, a Cambridge academic or department would lead, not OTOS — see 01 — Funding Route Summary.

Task list, per document: G/D/Ready ticks, Owner assignment, and the per-document note are live via Supabase — Dean and Gareth see each other's changes on refresh, no GitHub push needed. The document files themselves (content, copy) still need a save + push to update for both.
