Existing contacts are not cold targets

Dean already knows, or has met, local Public Health and CPFT pathway/service people. Do not treat them as unknown cold-discovery targets — treat them as potential support-letter, site-feasibility, pathway-validation and steering/advisory contacts (see 07). This list is about finding the academic, research and funding people who can sit above or alongside those existing local contacts and turn the opportunity into an eligible, fundable study.
