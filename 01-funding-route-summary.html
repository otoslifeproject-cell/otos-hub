Funding Route Summary

Three funding routes for OTOS Continuity™, mapped to what already exists, what each route requires, and where OTOS sits in each application.

PurposeOne-page orientation for anyone joining the funding conversation — academic lead, service partner or OTOS team.
StatusDraft v1, first pass

01

The ladder

OTOS Continuity™ has one evidence ladder and three funding routes sit on different rungs of it. Applying to the wrong rung at the wrong time is the main risk this pack is trying to prevent.

1
Pre-Pilot / First-50Feasibility and acceptability, non-clinical signals only, single site or small cluster — this is the natural evidence base for a Medical Research Council (MRC) Stage One application.
2
Pilot / larger proof of conceptFollows First-50 data. Likely MRC Stage Two, or an NIHR / SBRI route, subject to official eligibility confirmation.
3
Mechanism / mental-health systems researchContinuity, engagement and waiting-well evidence as a research question in its own right — the Wellcome Mental Health route.
4
Implementation / commercial scalingPublic Health, strategic partner, angel, AstraZeneca / Roche, Innovate UK — later, and out of scope for this pack.

03

What this pack does not do

It does not present OTOS Digital Ltd as an MRC lead applicant, does not present First-50 as proof of efficacy, and does not treat Wellcome as a route to fund product build. Each document in the pack is written to that boundary; see 05 — Governance Boundary Note and 06 — Evidence Anchors.
