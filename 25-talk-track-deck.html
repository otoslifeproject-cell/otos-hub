01 · The problem

Unmanaged ADHD is a well-documented route into addiction.

People self-medicate an untreated neurological condition — often with alcohol — long before anyone identifies ADHD as the driver. Once in addiction treatment, an ADHD assessment waiting list can trap them there: the root cause goes unaddressed while the addiction service treats the symptom it can see.

03 · What OTOS Continuity™ is

A non-clinical Continuity Management Layer.

It creates an agreed, non-clinical Continuity Thread — a visible record of the user journey, built entirely from consent, prompts, replies, silence, handoff receipts, attendance signals, drift signals and human-review events.

  • One person, one thread — every service keeps its own separate record, exactly as today
  • Partners see only the thread elements they are authorised to see
  • A surfaced signal goes to a named human on the existing care team — never actioned automatically

08 · Why OTOS is not the applicant

These routes are built for eligible research organisations — not a technology company acting alone.

  • Presenting OTOS as lead would misstate the routes' own eligibility logic, and weaken rather than strengthen an application
  • OTOS's contribution is the intervention and its lived-experience origin — not research infrastructure, ethics sponsorship or academic governance
  • OTOS does not hold the grant, does not sponsor ethics, and is not the accountable body to the funder
  • OTOS's commercial interest is disclosed as a matter of course, consistent with standard funder conflict-of-interest requirements
