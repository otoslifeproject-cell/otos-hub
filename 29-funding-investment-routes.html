Funding & Investment Routes

A working outreach map for angel, grant, investment and build-support routes — with owner and live status per route. Not a claim of investor interest, grant eligibility, partnership, NHS approval, endorsement or funding commitment from anyone named here. Verify every window, deadline and eligibility rule against the official source before acting; roles and calls change.

Operating boundary — how first contact works

Do not send the full pack cold. First contact stays small, private and route-checking: a short "bite" email or a route-check call. Only after a warm reply send one relevant PDF (angel version or grant/NHS version — never both unless asked), then ask for a 30-minute meeting. Do not pitch the full ~£150k ask until route fit is confirmed.

Core first ask: "Could I get 20–30 minutes to ask whether this NHS-adjacent continuity pilot is worth shaping properly for your funding / angel route?"

POSITIONING

Core positioning & red lines

OTOS Continuity is a lived-experience-led, non-clinical continuity infrastructure demonstrator for people at risk of being lost between ADHD, addiction, mental-health, community and recovery services. It does not replace those services. It gives them a shared, consented thread.
  • Do not claim OTOS has NHS approval.
  • Do not claim OTOS will reduce costs until tested — use "hypothesis" only.
  • Do not claim OTOS diagnoses, treats, prescribes, triages or makes clinical decisions.
  • Do not imply partner endorsement from CPFT, CGL, CRS, SUN, NHS, National Lottery, NIHR or any investor.
  • Do not send private founder material in first contact.
  • Do not send both PDFs unless asked.
  • Do not treat closed calls as open.
