The category, named

NIA — Neurologically Informed Addiction.

NIA — Neurologically Informed Addiction — is proposed terminology coined by Dean Butler / OTOS. It is not currently an official diagnosis. It names a real, under-recognised addiction pattern where underlying neurology — especially ADHD — can drive self-medication, poor treatment retention, relapse and recurring public cost.

The lived origin term was Double Trouble Diagnosis (DTD). NIA is the professional, AZ / NHS / investor-facing category that should now be researched, piloted and recognised — alongside, never replacing, existing diagnoses and services.

"I did not invent the suffering. I named the pattern."

Adult ADHD wave · breakwater

The wave is already being rationed.

Adult ADHD assessment and treatment in England are under acute strain. Demand is rising sharply; waiting lists run to years in places; and Integrated Care Boards, under hard financial pressure, have begun rationing access. From 21 May 2025, Coventry & Warwickshire paused new adult ADHD Right-to-Choose referrals for people aged 25 and over, to redirect capacity to children. Through late 2025 and into 2026, at least nine NHS areas instructed Right-to-Choose providers to pause new autism and ADHD assessments on funding grounds, under NHS England's 2025 activity-cap contracting.

When adult ADHD assessment and treatment capacity is restricted, the system does not remove the need. It stores it as future crisis. Adults with untreated or under-treated ADHD may self-medicate; some then cross into addiction, relapse, A&E, detox, crisis mental health, safeguarding, police, courts and prison. Because ADHD remains unrecognised, the system repeatedly treats the downstream manifestation while the upstream neurological driver remains untreated.

"The adult ADHD wave is already being rationed. The addiction cost wave may arrive later."
Evidence-safe framing

Publicly verified evidence shows local adult ADHD referral pauses, restrictions, prioritisation and at least one clear over-25 adult-access restriction (Coventry & Warwickshire). Dean's earlier "over-23" language is his risk-band / forecast language unless separately verified. The evidence does not yet prove a national "tsunami" — but it shows the conditions for one. OTOS is the breakwater: a Continuity Layer that helps hold the person while the clinical system catches up.

Why the model holds

What recovery needs. What ADHD takes away.

Line up what successful addiction recovery requires against what unmanaged ADHD impairs — not loosely, item for item. The treatment model and the disorder are pointed in opposite directions — which is why this cohort relapses through good support, and why medicine-plus-continuity starts the sequence with the driver.

CapacityUnmanaged ADHD impairsAddiction recovery requires
Executive functionDifficulty planning, sequencing and self-regulating; a starved prefrontal cortexDaily planning, structure and the delay of short-term gratification
Focus & attentionChronic distractibility; hard to sustain attention on non-stimulating tasksDeep, continuous focus through long therapy sessions and recovery work
Working memoryForgets coping strategies, misses appointments, loses the threadRetaining new tools, tracking triggers, holding daily routines
Impulse controlReflexive, immediate urges; acting before the pausePausing, reflecting and resisting cravings in the moment
Emotional regulationDysregulation; distress arrives fast and overwhelmsTolerating distress and riding out craving without the substance
Routine & follow-throughHard to initiate and sustain mundane, unrewarding routinesRepetition and routine that physically rebuild new pathways
Reward & arousalUnder-arousal and a pull toward immediate rewardA stable baseline — tolerating boredom and delayed reward
Neuroplastic learningBlunted adaptation; resists routines without immediate payoffRapid, healthy re-wiring to replace deep-seated habits

Read it as a model, not a verdict. Presentations vary and the alignment is directional — offered to show why this cohort struggles with standard recovery, not to diagnose any individual.

This is the mechanism case for medicine-plus-continuity: treat the driver first, and the recovery work — and the continuity layer around it — finally have something to hold.
