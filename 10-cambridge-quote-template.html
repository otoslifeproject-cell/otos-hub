Cambridge Academic Quote Template

Use this to ask an academic contact for a short, attributable quote for applications, decks or outreach — and to show them exactly what's being asked for.

A. Request message

Dear Professor [Name],

Following our conversation, would you be willing to provide a short quote (two to three sentences) we could use in funding applications and partner materials, on your view of the research question OTOS Continuity™ raises? You're welcome to redraft it entirely in your own words — the prompt below is only a starting point, and we won't use anything you haven't approved in the exact form you approve it.

B. Prompt to work from (optional)

"This raises a genuine, testable question about continuity and engagement in [pathway] — one that a properly designed feasibility study could answer." — starting prompt only; please rewrite freely

C. Fields to complete once a quote is agreed

Quote[Exact approved wording]
Name[Full name]
Title[Post and department]
Institution[University / NHS organisation]
Approved for use in[e.g. MRC Stage One application only / all partner materials]
Date approved[Date]
Rule

Never publish or submit a quote without written confirmation of the exact wording and the scope it's approved for. Re-confirm before reusing an old quote in a new document.
