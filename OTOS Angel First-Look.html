Founder-market fit
The gap between ADHD and addictionis where people get lost.

A large share of adults with untreated or under-treated ADHD self-medicate. Many find their way into addiction services; few get the ADHD half identified or treated in time. The gap between "you're on the list" and "you're being held" is where drift, relapse and devastation happen. We are already paying for this failure — just in the worst possible places. Dean recovered by engineering a route the system should have had. OTOS exists so the next person does not have to design their own rescue route from inside crisis. Founder proof is not population proof.

"Adult ADHD, previously complicated with excess alcohol use to self-medicate inadequately managed ADHD symptoms."Independent consultant psychiatrist — diagnostic letter to the CPFT ADHD Team, April 2026.

That sentence is the cohort. Not ADHD and alcohol use as two separate problems — adult ADHD, complicated by self-medication. The letter is medical correspondence about Dean — not an endorsement of OTOS, and not proof that OTOS works — but it documents that the mechanism is real in a lived pathway.

OTOS ContinuityThe idea
03
OTOS Continuity™
The intervention is simple:make the missing thread visible.

OTOS does not replace services. It makes the handoff, the waiting period and the drift between them visible enough for humans to act. Waiting is not neutral for this cohort: waiting without structure is where ADHD-driven drift becomes addiction-driven crisis — OTOS turns it into active continuity.

In plain terms, OTOS holds one person's continuity thread across the services already around them — surfacing simple signals like whether someone is still connected or starting to drift, whether a handoff actually happened, and whether the next step landed. Every signal sits behind human review; clinical teams keep every clinical decision.

The first product is not a cure. It is visibility — seeing whether the person is still held between services before crisis becomes the only signal. OTOS surfaces signals. Partner teams make the decisions.

OTOS ContinuityBoundary & governance
05
The line OTOS holds
Stated plainly,held to.
① Status

OTOS is early-stage, pre-pilot and non-clinical. NIA is proposed founder-originated terminology — not an official diagnosis.

② What OTOS does not do

Does not diagnose ADHD, prescribe, manage medication, treat addiction, deliver therapy, replace any NHS / CGL / CPFT / GP / psychiatry / recovery / VCSE / crisis service, make clinical decisions, or act as an EPR.

③ How evidence is framed

Founder proof is not population proof. Published figures are evidence candidates, not OTOS outcomes. The clinical letter is medical correspondence, not an endorsement.

④ Partner status

No partner endorsement, approved pilot or procurement is assumed unless explicitly confirmed in writing.

⑤ Governance

Any future work is subject to governance, consent, data protection, safeguarding, qualified clinical leadership and proper legal review.

⑥ The line

OTOS surfaces signals; partner teams make the decisions. That boundary is the design, not a disclaimer bolted on after.
